Represented the CompSci community in the 21st Century Techforce initiative

21st Century Techforce is an initiative by the Puerto Rico Department of Economic Development and Commerce aimed at boosting the island’s digital talent. Through a stackable credentials model, students can progressively earn certifications in computer science: a certificate in one year, an associate degree in two years, and a bachelor’s degree in four years.

I had the opportunity to collaborate with the Interamerican University of Puerto Rico, Arecibo Campus, in presenting the 21st Century Techforce initiative through a promotional video. Learn more about this initiative here: 21st Century Workforce.
